Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Our Return Policy

At Ayurvedic Wellness, we take great care in preparing and packaging our authentic Ayurvedic medicines. However, we understand that sometimes returns are necessary. Please review our policy below.

Important: Due to the nature of Ayurvedic products, we can only accept returns under specific conditions to ensure product safety and quality for all customers.

Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery for the following reasons:

  • Damaged or defective products (please provide photos)
  • Incorrect item received
  • Unopened products in original packaging

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns for:

  • Opened or used Ayurvedic medicines
  • Personalized or made-to-order formulations
  • Herbal teas or consumables that have been opened
  • Products purchased during sales or special promotions marked "final sale"

Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service within 15 days of delivery at returns@ayurvedicwellness.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. If approved, you'll receive return instructions
  4. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item back to us (return shipping at customer's expense)

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

Exchanges

If you received a defective or wrong item, we'll gladly exchange it for the correct product. Please follow the return process above and specify you'd like an exchange.

Damaged or Defective Products

If your Ayurvedic medicine arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately with photos of the product and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

Questions?
